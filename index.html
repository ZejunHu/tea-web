What is Pamela

Eternity bands are a classy and stylish innovation to storm the market.

Pamela, meaning “all sweetness”, signiﬁes the way we handle our tea leaves: with love and care. From Sri Lanka to your home, our tea brings bold and exotic ﬂavors that will renew your tea drinking experience. On top of the ﬂavor, Ceylon black tea provides enormous health beneﬁts, such as improving immunity and protect our heart. But what distinguishes Pamela? We value our workers, treat them with integrity, and in return, they devote to meticulously producing tea leaves that are processed by hand. Pamela promises you each cup will be refreshing and memorable.
